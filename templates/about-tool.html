Tremor

Slowness of movement Impaired balance and coordination, sometimes leading to falls

Trouble Sleeping

Do you thrash around in bed or act out dreams when you are deeply asleep? Sometimes, your spouse will notice or will want to move to another bed. Sudden movements during sleep may be a sign of Parkinson's disease.

Trouble Walking

Depression

Up to 50% of people with Parkinson's disease experience depression. These overwhelming and long-lasting feelings come from chemical changes in your brain.
